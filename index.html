How to Sign Up as a merchant at Zeend.com

1. Create a Merchant Account

  • Sign Up as a Zeend Merchant
  • Input store details (category, name, address)
  • Upload business permit and goverment issued ID
  • Input complete merchant information

2. Set Up your Products

  • Set Up products on your online store, Choose from:
    • Zeend Catalogue
    • My Product (upload your own product)

3. Manage your Store

  • Input complete store informtion
  • Review Product Catalogue
  • Add Personnel
  • Update Prices
  • View Orders

4. Congratulations! you are now ready to sell online!
